Strawberry Banana Breeze Smoothie

Homepage

*Recipe by Almond Breeze

Description

Smoothies are a timeless staple of summer gastronomy, and are best enjoyed by the fading light of a summer sunset

This recipe describes a basic strawberry banana smoothie, with added pineapple for tropical flavour

Ingredients

  • 1 medium banana
  • 1 and 1/2 cups fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup Almond Breeze almondmilk
  • 1/2 cup fresh or juice packed pineapple
  • 1 tablespoon slivered or sliced almonds (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon flax seeds (Optional)

Steps

  1. Puree all ingredients in a blender until smooth
